The Coffee Potter Review

The Coffee Potter

24 Schooleys Mountain Road Long Valley, NJ 07853

$5.50 Small

Provides a sweet taste that may be overpowering for the umami matcha enjoyer, but will have sweet matcha enjoyers immediately fall in love! There's an option to sweeten with honey and to add whipped cream for those who like it extra sweet.

Note: The day I took this photo they had given me an okay cup of matcha. Normally they're much better but they've been training new barista's lately that are still learning to make a good matcha. The matcha still tasted great, but it had those grainy chunks at the bottom of the drink that come as a result of not sifting the matcha thoroughly.
