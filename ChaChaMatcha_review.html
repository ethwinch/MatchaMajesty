MATCHA MAJESTY

Cha Cha Matcha Review

Cha Cha Matcha

501 5th Ave New York, NY 10017

$7.50 Small

This matcha was terrible, chalky, chunky, and grainy. It was akin to drinking sand. It was very bitter.

For a place whose whole theme is matcha, it's embarrassing that they don't know how to make a basic matcha latte.

I feel like I need to revisit this place because it's insane to me that they have so many locations and their whole shtick is matcha and yet it was one of the worst cups I've ever had.

Maybe it's just the Bryant Park location that's this bad, or maybe I had a bad barista that day. This place genuinely puzzles me. I need to revisit this place to confirm whether I am crazy or not.
