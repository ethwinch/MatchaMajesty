MATCHA MAJESTY

Isshiki Review

Isshiki

176 8th Ave, New York, NY 10011, United States

$7.00 Small

I've been so excited to try this place since I discovered it on Google Maps and saw its curated menu of Japanese tea! They not only have matcha, but also genmaicha and my other favs!

When I finally got to try their matcha, I was not disappointed! When I took the first sip my first thought was Matcha House- high quality matcha with a rich umami flavor.

They had really cute outdoor seating, decorated with pumpkins for fall and everything. I'm glad I came here when the weather was comfy, especially since there's no indoor seating.

The people around me seemed to be seasoned matcha lovers as we all had the habit of swirling our cups to mix the matcha before each sip. I felt like i was among fellow matcha snobs haha
