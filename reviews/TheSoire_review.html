Thē Soirēe Review

Thē Soirēe

796 9th Ave, New York, NY 10019

$7.75 Kaori | $8.75 Uji Small (~12oz)

Thē Soirēe offers two types of matcha (Kaori and Uji) with Uji being more expensive and flavorful.

Kaori Matcha

This matcha is better quality than others in the area, but not particularly flavorful. The milk overpowered the matcha flavor. I don't think it's worth the price.

Uji Matcha

The milk is not overpowering like in the Kaori matcha. The matcha flavor comes through as smooth and umami with a hint of bitterness. The matcha is higher quality than places nearby such as White Noise, though it's also more expensive than higher-quality places like Matcha House and Isshiki.

The first image is of the Kaori matcha, and the last two are of the Uji matcha.
