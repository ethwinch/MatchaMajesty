Flander's Bagels Review

Flander's Bagels

293 US-206, Flanders, NJ 07836

$5.18 Medium (only size available)

This is a milkshake; not a matcha latte. It's full of sugar leading to a saccharine taste and a thick texture. My mom tried a sip and said it tastes like grass, but all I taste is sugar.

I adore this place for its bagels, but I will never be buying matcha from here again.
