MATCHA MAJESTY

Yanni's Coffee Review

Yanni's Coffee

96 7th Ave, New York, NY 10011

$7.62

It doesnt taste like it was made with experience. The whole milk is prominent in the taste, but the quality of the matcha itself seems fine. It's an okay matcha, but the price is not worth it; otherwise id say it's "good in a pinch".

The matcha leans towards umami rather than sweet. It's really not bad, it's just not worth nearly $8. For that price I expect Matcha House or Isshiki quality.
