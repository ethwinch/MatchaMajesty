The Monkey Cup Review

The Monkey Cup

1965 Adam Clayton Powell Jr Blvd, New York, NY 10026

$5.75 Small

This matcha has a lightness to it and a subtle sweet taste. No notes, it's a great & refreshing cup of matcha. This has quickly become one of my top favorite matcha spots.

⚠ 10/17/2025

I've gone back here a few times within the span of a couple months and everytime I've come back their matcha and most of their other teas have been out of stock. They always say they'll have more by the end of the week but they never do. I'm not sure why they've been out of matcha for so long, but maybe wait a couple more months before trying this place out. They seem to be out of matcha, chai, and many other drinks.

⚠ 11/9/2025

They are still out of matcha.
