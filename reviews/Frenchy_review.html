MATCHA MAJESTY

Frenchy Review

Frenchy

129 E 102nd St, New York, NY 10029

$5.50 Small

This is my go-to matcha spot when I'm in East Harlem! The matcha has a light umami taste and is always smooth, though the flavor varies wildly depending on which barista is making it that day. Its flavor isn't as rich as the five star matchas, but it isn't watered down or poorly made like the three star matchas. It sits in this middle ground of not being too strong or too subtle.

I won't lie, I'm partial to this matcha since I would get it every day before work. I used to think it was the best matcha in NYC, but since trying Matcha House, Isshiki and the other five star matchas I've reviewed, I've noticed that Frenchy matcha, while still good, is not the best flavor out there. I would still recommend it though, especially for people new to matcha.
