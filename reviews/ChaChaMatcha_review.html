MATCHA MAJESTY

Cha Cha Matcha Review

Cha Cha Matcha

501 5th Ave New York, NY 10017

$6.00 12oz Small

This matcha was terrible, chalky, chunky, bitter and grainy. It was akin to drinking sand and the milk was overpowering the taste of the matcha.

For a place whose whole theme is matcha, it's embarrassing that they don't know how to make a basic matcha latte.

They made my matcha in a few seconds, which is a red flag since tea is not something you can rush. It takes time to sift and mix the matcha, which they neglected to do. They take it straight from a big tub of matcha and into the bowl. Instead of a chasen, they used a weird frother-chasen hybrid. All this results in a chunky, grainy, bitter taste with an inconsistant flavor in every sip. It was kind of like drinking bitter, pulpy orange juice. The matcha was so chunky that it was catching in my throat while drinking it!

There are people who swear by this matcha for some reason. When I was sitting in the cafe and drinking my matcha, I saw many people come in and out with the body language and practiced ease that implies this cafe is part of their daily routine, much to my shock. I had to come back twice to make sure I wasn't crazy because the dissonance between the reviews and reality was so stark.

Overall, this place has a cute aesthetic, but it doesn't go deeper than that.
