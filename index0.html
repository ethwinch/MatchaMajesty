It's time to spill the tea on matcha! Since matcha's rise in popularity many places have tried to cash in on the hype. Unfortunately, most of these places never bothered to learn how to make a decent cup. After way too many bad matchas and bad recommendations from people who love the flavor of the syrup more than that of the matcha, I began rating matcha in the notes app on my phone. Now, these ratings are on this website for all to see!

Search the map below to see the places I've reviewed! So far, I've only reviewed places in NYC and NJ. Hopefully I can branch out to other states some day.

Important Note: My reviews are based on the natural matcha flavor in latte form. This means I don't buy lattes with lavendar, strawberry, vanilla or any other kind of sweetener. I order the standard matcha latte which sometimes comes already sweetened with agave or honey, but I take the unsweetened option if they inform me that they normally add sweetener by default. The point is to review the flavor of the MATCHA, not the sweetener.

Also, I am not a matcha expert! I'm just a matcha lover sharing my matcha related thoughts and opinions on my own humble corner of the internet. If you disagree with my rating, that's fine, just be respectful! ‚ô°
