MATCHA MAJESTY

Ground Central Coffee Company Review

Ground Central Coffee Company

888 8th Ave New York, NY 10019

$5.40 Small

They really fill the cup to the brim! The matcha itself is decent with a very mild umami flavor. The matcha taste isn't very strong, but it's there.

There's not much to say. The matcha isn't particularly special, but the taste isn't offensive so it's a good way to get your caffeine fix.

If you need caffeine and want a quick matcha that's not too fancy or strong, this is a good choice.
