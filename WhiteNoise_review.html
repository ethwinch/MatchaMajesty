MATCHA MAJESTY

White Noise Review

White Noise

829 11th Ave New York, NY 10019

$6.00 Small

A light, sweet taste, but a bit chalky and low quality. They put a lot of ice in their drinks, so your $6 doesn't go very far.

Most of the flavor seems to come from the sweetener they use. It comes sweetened by default, but I also tried it unsweetened. I wouldn't recommend getting this latte without the sweetener, as I don't think the matcha is of good enough quality to stand on its own.

I went back a couple more times and a more experienced barista made my drink one of the days. On that day, the matcha and milk flavors blended together better, the sweetness was more subtle, and the chalkyness wasn't as prominent. It made for a light, smooth taste.

That's one of the downsides with matcha; the flavor and texture varies wildly based on the skill of the person making it that day.

Overall, this is the only place in that area that I've found value in going back to. When I need a matcha between classes, this is the cafe I go to!
