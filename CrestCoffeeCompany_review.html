MATCHA MAJESTY

Crest Coffee Company Review

Crest Coffee Company

104 E Cardinal Rd, Wildwood Crest, NJ 08260

$5.50 Small

A light, refreshing taste with a hint of sweetness. It comes sweetened by default (it tasted like it was sweetened with agave rather than honey). I wish it had a stronger matcha taste as the matcha flavor is barely there; It tastes watered down. Overall I'd say this is the best matcha in Wildwood Crest.
