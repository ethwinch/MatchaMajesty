MATCHA MAJESTY

Matcha House Review

Matcha House

203 E 10th St, New York, NY 10003

$7.25 Small

The baristas really know what they're doing here! This is a good place to go if you are a matcha veteran or beginner, as they quality is great & consistant and the baristas are very good at explaining the taste of their matcha.

This matcha latte gives a light aroma. The first time I went, my matcha was sweetened without my knowledge since they use a bit of sweetener by default. The sweetness is barely noticable though so the rich nutty flavor of the matcha still shines through.

If you prefer a more mild matcha that's not too umami or sweet, you should get it with sweetener. They also have a bottle of sweetener out for customers to use to adjust the sweetness level themselves.

Originally, I was worried this would be another Cha Cha Matcha situation where their whole brand is matcha but they cant make a good cup, but im happy to say it's the exact opposite! They know what theyre doing here, and I've gotten into the habit of stoping by whenever I'm in the area.
